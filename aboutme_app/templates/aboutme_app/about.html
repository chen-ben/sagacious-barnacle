About

I once met someone who was on Family Feud and it made me realize most people revel in knowing what the masses know—after all, we’ve created television game shows dedicated to promoting and rewarding mainstream thinking, testing individuals to answer:

"When approached with a question or issue, how would most people respond?"

I realized from an early age that I wasn't interested in games like these—I wanted to go beyond understanding the motivations behind the obvious. I want to spearhead creative solutions that can solve the problems of the present and the future, drive these initiatives from start to finish, and reflect on my work throughout the development process.

In short, I'm continually challenging myself to answer the following:

"How can I anticipate upcoming trends and use them to solve to real-world problems?"

I've spent the past few years cultivating that creative energy into a more streamlined, systematic approach and I'm ready to apply myself to my next calling.

Let's connect,
